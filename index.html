PING-PONG GAME

How To Play
Basic rules and how the game game works.

Its nice having fun and getting to relax your mind with a small game of numbers. The game works quite simply. Here are the guidelines to the game: Upon input of a number the following happen;

  • Numbers divisible by 3 become "ping"
  • Numbers divisible by 5 become "pong"
  • Numbers divisible by 15 become "pingpong"
Fantastic!!! Now here's how to earn points after entering your number and clicking the play button:
  • When your result is the number you keyed in you earn 0 points
  • When your result is "ping" you earn 5 points
  • When your result is "pong" you earn 10 points
  • When your result is "pingpong" you earn 15 points
There you go! Play as many times as you can and add up your points to see how Mathematicized you are!!!Good Luck!!
